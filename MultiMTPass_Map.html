Multi-Mountain Passes Map

This map is designed for skiers and snowboarders who either already have a multi-resort pass or are deciding which one to purchase. Whether you’re planning your next trip using a pass you’ve already bought or comparing pass options based on mountain access, this map highlights all medium to large ski areas in New York, Vermont, and New Hampshire that are part of the Ikon, Epic, or Indy Pass networks as of 2025.

Note

If you want to learn more about pass pricing and partner mountains, keep scrolling!

Indy Pass

Visit The Website

Indy Base Pass: Two days at each of the 250+ mountains included, with a 25% discount available for a third day. Black out dates apply.

Age Price
Adults $299
Kids $179

Indy+ Pass: Two days at each of the 250+ mountains included, with a 25% discount available for a third day. No Black out dates.

Age Price
Adults $349
Kids $199

**Note: Pricing is subject to change based on when the pass is purchased.
